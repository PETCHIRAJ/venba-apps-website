Overview

Venba Apps ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our mobile applications.

Apps Covered

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications published by Venba Apps, including:

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our apps (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash Reports: Information about app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)

Information You Provide

  • App Data: Information you enter in the app (stored locally on your device)

How We Use Information

  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with our apps
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send app-related communications (if applicable)

  • Protect against fraudulent or illegal activity

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly agree
  • Service providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate our apps (Firebase, AdMob)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Storage & Security

  • Personal data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • We don't store sensitive financial information on our servers
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • We use industry-standard security measures

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt-out of analytics collection (if available in app)
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data:

  • Email us at contact.venbaapps@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include the app name and any account identifiers (if applicable)
  • We will process your request within 30 days
  • Note: Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes

Data Retention

We retain data for different periods:

  • Analytics Data: As per service provider policies
  • Crash Reports: As needed for debugging
  • Support Emails: As needed
  • Local App Data: Until you delete the app

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our apps, you consent to such transfers.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.
