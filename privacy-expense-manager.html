Anonymous Crash Reporting

To improve app stability, we collect anonymous crash reports through Firebase Crashlytics. These reports:

  • Contain NO personal information or expense data
  • Include only technical details about the crash (stack traces, device model, OS version)
  • Cannot be traced back to you
  • Help us fix bugs and improve the app for everyone

Important: Your expense data is NEVER included in crash reports. Only technical error information is collected to help us improve app stability.

Data Storage & Security

SQLCipher Encryption

All your expense data is protected with SQLCipher encryption. Your data is encrypted at rest on your device, making it unreadable even if someone gains physical access to your phone.

  • Storage Location: Your device's secure app storage only
  • Encryption: SQLCipher database encryption
  • Backup: Standard Android/iOS device backups (encrypted)
  • Access: Only you, through the app on your device

Optional Premium Features

üíé Premium Users Only (Optional)

Premium users can optionally choose to:

  • Google Drive Backup: Your data is encrypted before upload and remains encrypted in your personal Google Drive. We never have access to this backup.
  • CSV Export: Export your expenses for use in spreadsheets. This is entirely user-initiated and controlled.

Important: These features are 100% optional. You can use premium features without ever enabling cloud backup.
